Admission Procedure

Eligibility for Admission

B.Sc.CSIT

  • Minimum ‘C’ grade in all subjects in PCL/+2 in Science (Physics or Biology)
  • PASSED in TU B.Sc. CSIT Entrance Exam

BIM

  • Minimum CGPA 1.8 in grade 11 and 12 or minimum score of second division marks in 10+2, PCL or Equivalent in any discipline.
  • PASSED in CMAT Test

BBM

  • Minimum ‘C’ grade in all subjects in PCL/+2
  • PASSED in CMAT Test

BCA

  • Minimum 2 CGPA with at least ‘D+’ grade in all subjects in PCL/+2
  • PASSED in BCA Entrance Test

Entrance Exam Application Form

University Entrance Exam Application forms are available at ASMT and all the colleges which offer the programs of B.Sc. CSIT, BIM, BBM & BCA.

Entrance exam

Getting through the University Entrance Exam is mandatory for admission in B.Sc. CSIT, BIM, BBM & BCA.

ASMT Admission Form

Successful students in the University Entrance Exam should fill in the ASMT Admission Form. Students who have filled in the University Entrance Exam forms in any other college are also eligible to apply here.

Personal Interview

These students need to appear for the Personal Interview immediately thereafter. They ought to be accompanied by their parents or guardians for this interview.

Offer Letter

Selected applicants are handed Offer Letters for admission. They need to submit their acceptance along with needed original certificates or testimonials within the stipulated period to get admitted.

Enrollment

Once the College receives a letter of acceptance, it will confirm admission forthwith payment of fees.
